# Agile Scrum

This harness uses Scrum ideas pragmatically.

- Product Backlog: epics and story candidates.
- Sprint Backlog: stories that pass Story Gate.
- Definition of Ready: product contract, dependencies, design notes, validation proof.
- Definition of Done: implementation, tests, docs, decisions, test matrix, and evidence are current.
- Sprint Review: demo behavior against acceptance criteria.
- Retrospective: capture harness friction and improve the process.
